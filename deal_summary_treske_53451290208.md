- **Hardware:** Neptune High Performance (180 kV)

- **Voyager Features Required:** Dimensional measurement, 2D dimensioning, 3D auto-dimensioning (beta/upcoming), GD&T suite (in development), analysis recipes for automation

### Technical Assessment Readout (TAR) Results
- **Sample Part Scanned:** Aluminum machined part (non-ITAR sample sent for evaluation)
- **Scan Quality:** Overnight scan and 2-hour scan both showed no difference in noise level – excellent quality on aluminum
- **Voxel Size Achieved:** 27.5 microns (1.8 thou) for full part scan
- **Dimensioning Accuracy:** 1/10th of voxel size = ~0.1 thou (2.75 microns)
- **Measurements Taken:** All required measurements from 3 drawing perspectives completed successfully in ~15 minutes setup
- **Thread Inspection:** CT can visualize thread form and major diameter
- **Through-hole Diameter Validation:** CT measurement of 0.2522" matched expected pre-plate dimension of 0.2522" – validated accuracy
- **Key Finding:** High-tolerance callouts (e.g., 0.0008" tolerance) are challenging for CT as a technology and better suited to stay on CMM. CT excels for features within 0.1 thou accuracy.

### Technical Capabilities Demonstrated
1. 2D dimensioning with edge-detected line and circle snapping
2. Search region-based measurement for automated/repeatable analysis
3. Beta 3D auto-dimensioning with surface extraction (coming in ~1 month from Feb 2026)
4. GD&T suite beta: parallelism, datums, pass/fail tolerance criteria
5. CAD overlay / color-by-deviation analysis
6. Thread form visualization
7. Multi-part fixturing potential
8. Recipe-based automated analysis workflows
